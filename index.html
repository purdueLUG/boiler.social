welcome to boiler.social

boiler.social provides a Matrix homeserver for use by Purdue students, faculty, etc. Hosting is physically on campus.

To sign up, simply direct your Matrix client of choice to use boiler.social as your homeserver. We'd sugguest using Element for new users of Matrix.

use policy

Do not create rooms which violate Purdue's policies on e.g. academic integrity.

Do not violate the stated policies (if any) of rooms you join, or otherwise be a nuisance to homeserver administrators.

A blanket ban on racism, homophobia, transphobia, discrimination based on major, etc applies to any room published by boiler.social.

In summary: Don't make us regret providing this service. The Administration reserves the right to terminate service to any user.

on privacy/logging

Access to homeserver logs is restricted to administrators of boiler.social.

We have not been asked to provide sensitive information (e.g. logs, TLS keys, password hashes) to law enforcement, government agencies, or other third parties.

The Matrix protocol leaves the security of some encryption keys up to the end-user. Please encrypt responsibly.

We are based in (and ergo regulated by) the state of Indiana within the United States of America.

We are hosted by ITaP; you may find their privacy policy here.

Requests to matrix.boiler.social, i.e. the actual homeserver, are handled directly. Requests to boiler.social (static content such as this page and .well-known) are proxied through CloudFlare's CDN.
